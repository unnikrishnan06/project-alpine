Driving Innovation: The Future of Renault.

We are united by a shared commitment to excellence and innovation, extending across our employees, brands, businesses, and partners. Together, we are shaping a safer, more inclusive, and more sustainable future. With 125 years of proud history behind us, our Group leads the way in driving progress and transformation in mobility.

Founded in 1899 by the Renault brothers, Louis, Marcel, and Fernand, Renault quickly gained a reputation for producing high-quality, reliable cars and has played a significant role in the automotive industry for more than a century. Committed to innovation, Renault is known for its advancements in automotive technology, particularly in electric vehicles (EVs), autonomous driving, and connectivity. The company has been at the forefront of the transition to electric mobility, with models like the Renault Zoe becoming one of Europe's best-selling electric cars. Sustainability is deeply integrated into Renault's core values, focusing on reducing emissions, developing energy-efficient vehicles, and recycling materials in manufacturing. The company is also improving urban mobility through electric and hybrid models. Operating in over 120 countries and with production facilities worldwide, Renault is part of the Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi Alliance, enhancing its ability to innovate and compete globally. Looking to the future, Renault is heavily investing in new mobility solutions, such as electric cars, shared mobility, and autonomous driving technologies, with the vision of offering vehicles that make life simpler, safer, and more enjoyable while reducing environmental impact.
